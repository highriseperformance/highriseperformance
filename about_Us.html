Who are we?

Looking for a professional in-home personal trainer? Then look no further than High Rise Performance. An exciting company that was developed and engineered by Chicago born Antonio Garcia III. While working as a certified personal trainer for an accredited company in Chicago, Antonio realized his skill set could be put to better use creating his own business and giving a more personal and tailored program to Chicago residents.

Here at High Rise Performance, we look to serve those who desire to be trained where it is convenient for them. Our services are perfect for those who do not want to work out in a commercial gym or in front of crowds. We are an inclusive company, who can tailor our programs to benefit people of all ages, fitness levels, and abilities. Don’t know how to perform any exercises? Need adjustments due to underlying conditions or injury? Maybe some exercises that can be performed at home or in the office? Perhaps your schedule is so busy that you don’t have time to travel to a gym. No worries, we come to you!

We want to serve everyday Chicagoans. Planning workouts, learning new exercise techniques, and tracking progress is a daunting task for a beginner. This process takes a mental toll and we are here to ease that burden. We customize all workout programs according to your goals and supervise each session to make sure you are performing exercises correctly. All you need to do is be prepared to follow instructions, and we’ll take care of the rest.

To us, fitness isn’t just about having a toned physique with rippling muscles. Fitness is about how healthy your body is. Is your cardiovascular system strong enough to handle the challenges life throws at you? Will you be able to carry your grandkids upstairs when they fall asleep? Can you keep up with your children during that pickup basketball game? We want to prepare you for the ups and downs that come with life. Just like you need a certain level of fitness to operate during tough times, you also need enough fitness to fully enjoy all the great times coming your way.
